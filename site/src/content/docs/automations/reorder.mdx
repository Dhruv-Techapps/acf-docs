<Callout type="info">
  Priority matters: when multiple automations match the same URL, the first match in the list wins.
</Callout>

Steps:

1. Click `Reorder` from the My Automations screen.
2. Drag and drop items to adjust their order.
3. Click Save to apply changes, or Close to discard.
