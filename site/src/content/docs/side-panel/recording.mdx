Record clicks, inputs, and interactions from the Side Panel recording tab and build your automation live.

## Steps

1. Open the extension Side Panel and go to `Recording`.
2. The Side Panel has two tabs:
   - `Automations` tab: your automation list. Click an automation to run it.
   - `Recording` tab: recording controls and captured steps.
3. Click `Start Recording` in the `Recording` tab.
4. Continue interacting with the page as usual (click, fill, select, etc.).
5. Each recorded step is saved immediately as it is performed. You do not need to wait for `Stop Recording` to persist steps.
6. Click `Stop Recording` when you are done to end the recording session.

<Callout type="info">
  Recording runs through the Side Panel workflow. Stopping recording ends capture, but step persistence happens
  continuously during recording.
</Callout>

See [Side Panel]([[docsref:/side-panel/overview]]) for opening methods, automation run behavior, and footer links.
